Problem:

Given 2 numbers a and b,You need to print the sum of those 2 given numbers.

Input:

First line contains T,the number of testcases,followed by T lines containing 2 space separated integers.
T,a,b < 10^6

Time-Limit:

1 sec

Output:

Resultant Sum of 2 numbers.

Example-Input:

3
1 2
5 10
6 8

Example-Output:

3
15
14
